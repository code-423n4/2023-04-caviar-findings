#### Gas-5 Functions guaranteed to revert can be made payable 

Compiler will not make checks if payment was made resulting in fewer OPCODES. Marking functions as payable that are guaranteed to revert if not called by right person lowers the gas costs for rightful caller e.g onlyAdmin, onlyOwner access controlled functions.
